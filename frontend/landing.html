About Us

A Gurukul rooted in
tradition, built for today

Kalashree Sangeet Gurukul is a premier Indian classical music academy based in Indira Nagar, Nashik, Maharashtra. Founded and led by an accomplished musician, the academy is built on the vision of preserving and propagating the rich heritage of Hindustani classical music.

We offer structured learning for students of all ages and levels — from young beginners to advanced disciples. Every student is guided personally in the true spirit of the Gurukul tradition.

What We Offer

Our Courses

Vocal Music

Beginner to Advanced

Classical vocal training in Hindustani style — from basic swaras and alankaars to complex ragas and bandishes.

Harmonium

Beginner to Advanced

Structured harmonium training covering scale practice, raga accompaniment and solo performance techniques.

Tabla

Beginner to Intermediate

Learn the art of tala and rhythm through structured tabla training — from basic bols to complex compositions.

Theory and Ragas

All Levels

In-depth study of raga grammar, aroha-avaroha, tala systems and the cultural history of classical music.
